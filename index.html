Hello!

I’m Thomas Laukkanen, a frontend developer who has passion for both the design part and coding part of creating attractive frontends to websites. I believe a website can look both amazing and still be functional at the same time, if the right amount of care is put in to it.

In 2020 i started my journey to pursue a career in the web development space after a long career in the car-industry. Im currently studying frontend development at It-högskolan in Gothenburg, Sweden. Where im learning the most import topics i need, to become an effective developer. I have always been drawn to things related to computers and with my curiosity i have learned many different topics, that i think can be useful as a programmer. On my spare time i like to train MMA or produce electronic music. But now i would say that my main hobby is to become the best developer i can be. As i really enjoy it!
